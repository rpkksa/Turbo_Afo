Ankle-foot orthotics are medical devices that are prescribed by a doctor to relieve strain or stabilize joints, tendons, or ligaments, or to increase mobility 1. One such company that specializes in manufacturing and fitting ankle-foot orthotics is Boston Orthotics & Prosthetics 2. They have expertise in treating defects and abnormalities in the lower legs due to congenital abnormalities, disease, injury, trauma, or other conditions 2. At Boston O&P, they understand that every patient is unique and has different needs. They offer a wide range of ankle-foot orthoses (AFOs) that are custom-made to fit each patient’s individual requirements 2. Their team of certified orthotists works closely with the patient’s doctor to ensure that the device is designed to meet their specific needs 2. Boston O&P’s mission is to provide the highest quality orthotic and prosthetic care to their patients. They are committed to using the latest technology and techniques to ensure that their patients receive the best possible care 2. They also offer a range of other orthotic and prosthetic devices for upper and lower limbs, including knee-ankle-foot orthoses (KAFOs), hip-knee-ankle-foot orthoses (HKAFOs), and spinal orthoses 2.
